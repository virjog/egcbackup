Society Funding

The two following documents are important for societies requesting funding for next semester:

Funding Packet (Document)
Funding Packet (Spreadsheet)

The Engineering Governing Council (EGC) provides funding to recognized Societies through the Finance Commitee. The Chair of the Finance Committee, Treasurer Jonathan C. Bernardo, can be contacted by email.

The Commitee uses Allocation Guidelines and the "Point System" to allocate funds in a viewpoint neutral manner.

Mid-Semester Funding

Mid-Semester funding is funding that is in addition to the semesterly funding provided by the EGC Finance Committee. The Committee reviews requests for mid-semester funding at their committee meetings. Please see Section VI of the allocation guidelines for more information. If your society wishes to request mid-semester funding for an event or project, please contact the Finance Committee.
