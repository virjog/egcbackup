Spring Elections
Congratulations to the 2007 – 2008 Engineering Governing Council Executive Board and Class Representatives!

The voter turnout was as follows:

Executive Board Elections: 295 voters (approximately 13.2% turnout)
Class of 2008 Representatives: 60 voters (approximately 9.9% turnout)
Class of 2009 Representatives: 86 voters (approximately 15.2% turnout)
Class of 2010 Representatives: 150 voters (approximately 24.8% turnout)
