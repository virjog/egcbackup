EGC Info

The Engineering Governing Council is the representative body for the students of the Rutgers University School of Engineering. Working in conjunction with the faculty and administration of the School to represent and further the students needs, desires, and best interests academically as well as University administration and the other Governing Associations to advance the School of Engineering's interests in the University as a whole.

Out recent actions have included:

  • Reviewing the Task Force Report and its effects on the students of the School of Engineering, then acting to recommend changes accordingly.
  • Working with the Rutgers College Governing Association to comprimise the fair distribution of student fees monies.

The EGC Council is comprised of

  • (1) President [Executive Board]
  • (1) Internal Vice President [Executive Board]
  • (1) External Vice President [Executive Board]
  • (1) Secretary [Executive Board]
  • (1) Treasurer [Executive Board]
  • (3) University Senator [Executive Board]
  • (5) Class Representatives from each class year (20 total)
  • (1) Society Representative from each recognized society
A Parliamentarian is internally elected at the first meeting after Spring Elections. The Council also elects and/or appoints representatives and envoys to other organizations.

Anyone is welcome to join us for all Council and Committee meetings.
