Starting a Society

One of the Engineering Governing Council's main functions is to help engineering related societies start, grow, and expand.

Interested in Starting an Engineering Society?

The requirements for a new Society are:
- 10 Members, half of whom are School of Engineering students
- A faculty advisor
- A Constitution (Our Internal Review Committee can help you with this)
- Approval by the Society Affairs Committee and the Engineering Governing Council

Please click here to view a suggested timeline and guidelines on this process.

Please contact the Chairman of the Society Affairs Committee, Eleanor Okubor, to set up a meeting for more information.
