Fall Elections '07
Elections for Representatives from the Class of 2011 will be held in September. In order to run for a class representative position, one must first petition their classmates and get 25 signatures endorsing their name to be put on the ballot. The petitioning period will last for seven days, at the end of which, the petitions will be collected. The following week has a three day online election period. The actual dates look as follows:
9/14-9/21 Petitioning Period
9/21 Petitions Due
9/24-9/26 Online Elections
Tip: Attending the mandatory Freshman Convocation is a great idea for meeting, petitioning, and campaigning among your classmates.
