There will be a Society Presidents Meeting on October 12 from 11:30 am to 3:30 pm. The main focus of this meeting is for each society to discuss its tasks and goals. It also serves the purpose of allowing the presidents to get to know one another, and Vice Presidents must also attend. The meeting will take place in BCC GSL (Rm. 174), and the attire is business casual. Lunch will be served.
