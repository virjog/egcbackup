Eric Viernes, a School of Engineering student and fellow EGC member, unexpectedly passed away on Monday, November 3, 2008. Dean Brown, Associate Dean of the School of Engineering, announced, "This sudden and untimely death was a result of a suicide which has shocked his family, friends and fellow students here at Rutgers.  Eric was a Mechanical Engineering Student who was scheduled to graduate in May 2009 and had been active in the Engineering Governing Council, ASME and other students organizations." Eric will truly be missed.
